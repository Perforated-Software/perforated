Pong 2 F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pong 2
​

Q: Why are there so many balls?
     A: You are playing in "Arcade Mode" you can toggle it off in the settings or main menus.

 Q: How do I open it?
     A: Pong 2 is opened by going to the apps section of chrome, or the app drawer on a Chromebook.

Q: Do I need an internet connection?
     A: An internet connection is only required to play online multiplayer. 

 Q: How do I play?
     A: Player 1 (left) uses W & A to move their paddle, and Player 2 (right) uses the arrow keys. You can use either while in
singleplayer.

 Q: How do I play online?
     A: You can play against a random opponent by selecting Online Multiplayer>Casual>Play, if you want to play competitively you can select Competitive instead.

 ​Q: How do I play with friends?
     A: To play with friends, go to Online Multiplayer>Casual then set a room name (can be anything unique) then click play, your friend should do the same (with the same room name) and you should then be in the same game.
​
 Q: I found a bug, what should I do?
     A: You can send in bug reports on the Chrome Store HERE. Bug reports are how we know there is a problem, so if you find a bug try reporting it.
